40Hz&H20 is a spatial audio performance inspired by research about
healing properties of the 40Hz tone in conjunction with the life-giving
properties of water. We aim to challenge common Western
misappropriation of the ‘sound bath’. 40Hz&H20 explores
possibilities within the realm of space and frequency,
aiming to create an inspiring, soothing space for
participants
